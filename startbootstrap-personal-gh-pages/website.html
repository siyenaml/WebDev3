Learn How to Dance Bachata

First, let's gather the things we need
  1. Comfortable clothes
    • Wear something you can move around in
    • Make sure you can sweat in these clothes
  2. Dance shoes or sneakers
    • Shoes from a dance shoe manufacturer are the best, however they are not cost friendly
    • For beginners, sneakers with a sole that is not grippy are a great option
  3. A partner (optional)
    • There is more variety in dancing with another person, however you can dance solo if you prefer
    • If you have a partner, make sure to designate a lead and a follow (typically males are leads and females are follows)
